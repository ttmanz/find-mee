Child Safety Standards

Last updated: July 2026

Find - Mee is a social platform designed exclusively for adults aged 18 and older. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and have zero tolerance for any content or behaviour that exploits, harms, or endangers children.

Age Requirement

Users must be 18 years of age or older to create an account or use Find - Mee. During registration, users confirm they meet this age requirement. Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately terminated.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Find - Mee strictly prohibits any content, behaviour, or communication that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE), including:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAE content

Any such content is immediately removed and reported to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

Reporting

Users can report any suspected CSAE content or behaviour directly within the app or by emailing support@find-mee.com. All reports are reviewed promptly and acted upon.

Enforcement

Violations of these standards result in immediate account termination, reporting to law enforcement, and cooperation with any investigation. We work proactively to prevent minors from accessing our platform.

Contact

For questions about our child safety standards or to report a concern, contact us at support@find-mee.com.
